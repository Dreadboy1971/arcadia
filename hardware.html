The Hardware

The PC hardware consists of the following:

  • 120V/250V AT Power Supply 250W
  • Gigabyte Pentium TX PCI/ISA Motherboard
  • Genuine Intel Pentium 233MMX Processor & Fan
  • 32MB RAM (60ns SIMMs)
  • Creative Labs Sound Blaster 64 AWE (16-Bit ISA)
  • NE2000-Compatible Network Card (ISA)
  • Creative Labs 36x EIDE CD-ROM drive
  • Western Digital 10GB UDMA/2 Hard Drive
  • STB Velocity 128 PCI TV 4MB Video Card (Produces standard NTSC 60Hz RGB signal directly out of 15-Pin VGA Port. No need for crappy composite connection and expensive composite to RGB converters.)
  • Logitech Trackman Marble 3-Button Trackball
  • Hacked Generic Keyboard (with a defective "M" key)
  • 2 Gravis Gamepad Controllers (Wired to joysticks and first two action buttons for each player.)

Nothing too fancy here except for the sound card. Basically a whole system with no monitor or floppy drive, I guess.
