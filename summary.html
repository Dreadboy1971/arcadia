Summary

What you'll have to do to get your cabinet working:

 

Method 1 - Installing PC Externally (Good for using your PC for multi-purpose, or if you're just a cheapskate.)

Basically the same as below, except you will want to extend these four connectors/cables out of the cabinet:

  • Video
  • Sound
  • Keyboard
  • Joysticks (If any)
